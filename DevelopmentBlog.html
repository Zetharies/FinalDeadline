AN INTRODUCTION TO THE TEAM AND GAME
01/12/2018
By Nisha Khatri

Welcome to the Final Dead-Line Development Blog, which will be updated with the development of our game and how the team is progressing.

Background

We decided to use libGDX to build our zombie-survival themed adventure game! The premise of the game is a zombie outbreak in a university where the player must complete tasks and puzzles to progress to the next level and save the university from the zombies!

The Team

So far, we have created a hack which represents only a small part of our game. We are now working on building multiple maps, creating puzzles and incorporating dialogue into the game. In order to achieve this, we have delegated roles to each team member, however this is not a rigid structure as team members can move around the departments if need be.

The End Goal

Voiceovers

Customised voiceovers depending on the character chosen to play and the characters that you interact with

An Immersive Story

A quiet day at university turns into a fight to save the university from a zombie outbreak, including zombie fights, puzzles and engaging dialogue with other characters

Comfortable Gameplay

An easy to use and intuitive gameplay, giving the player a choice of WASD or arrow keys

Customization

Allows player to choose from premade or a customised character

Challenging Levels

Includes five levels that increases in difficulty with the puzzles the player completes

Weaponry

Weaponry includes using objects found in the university to attack the zombies
